About Cats Curios

Meet the Cat

Hi. I’m Professor Pawsworth—part-time nap technician, full-time mystery investigator, and the self-appointed historian of every suspicious rustle, sunbeam, and snack packet in the neighbourhood.

Known For

  • Conducting nightly “perimeter checks” (also called zoomies)
  • Cataloguing boxes by comfort rating
  • Detecting tuna from three rooms away

Current Research

  • Why the red dot refuses to be caught
  • Whether the sofa is secretly moving at night
  • How many treats is “enough” (answer: more)

Every post is filed as an “official report” from my investigations—mood included. Please read responsibly and offer snacks where appropriate.

Cats Curios is a fictional blog built to practice modern Django development. Each post is a small “adventure” from a curious cat, with moods, images, and community comments.

Technologies

  • Django
  • Bootstrap 5
  • Cloudinary (media storage)
  • WhiteNoise (static files)

Learning goals

  • Authentication (login/logout/register)
  • CRUD patterns with class-based views
  • Relational data (posts + comments + profiles)
  • Deployment-ready configuration

Disclaimer: This is a student/portfolio project and the content is fictional.
